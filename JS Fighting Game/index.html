Movement (Player 2):
- move left and right with LeftArrow and RightArrow keys
- jump with the UpArrow key and crouch with the DownArrow key
Attacking:
- attack with the backslash (/) key

.........................

General Tips:
- characters have two jumps: one on the ground and one midair, jump refreshes when touching the ground
- crouching shrink your character's vertical hitbox by half
Attacking:
- attacks have attack lag (lag after attacking) and knockback (where opponent cannot move)
- attacking precisely can lead to combos
