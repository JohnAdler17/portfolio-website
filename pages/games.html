Fight! JS
A two player in-browser fighting game! Use your attacks to take out the opponent's health before the time runs out! Made entirely with javascript, CSS, and HTML5 canvas.
Starship Uprising
Take control of the Y10 starship and go to war against the galactic overlords! Fight hordes of enemy starships to take back control of the galaxy! The more enemy ships you destroy, the greater impact you have on the war, so try to get a high score! Either play the 30 hand-crafted levels or test your mettle in the Endless mode!
Smashdown Randomizer
Randomize smash bros. characters and remove them from the roster! Available modes are Iron Man, 3v3, 5v5, and 10v10! You can also select which smash bros game is being played to change the characters in the initial roster!
Quiz Master
Welcome to Quiz Master! Use your large or small brain to complete these goofy quizzes about random media! See how high a score you can get!
Boards with Bombs
Play three classic 1v1 games normally or with bombs! Games include Tic-Tac-Toe, Connect 4, and Hangman. Bombs act differently in each game, try to best your opponent with strategic bomb placement and usage!
Conquer the Cave
Take control of the explorer and make your way out of the dangerous cave! Make use of the explorer's roll and double jump to get through difficult platforming challenges! Can you beat all 24 levels and conquer the cave? Go for best times, least deaths, or a high score! Many levels have different paths, so see what works best for you!
